AI Ping Pong




Designed by - Diti uwu

Instructions

1. First keep your device still on a surface.
2.Move yourself approximately 3-4 feet away from your device.
3.Move your right wrist up or down on the screen, this is what controls your paddle. A red dot shall appear on your right wrist.
4.Now press the play button and move back to the spot where you were just standing.
5.The red paddle belongs to you and the other one is controled by the computer. The red paddle will move according to your right wrist.
